Our Story

Founded in 2010, Savoria has been delighting guests with exceptional Bangladeshi and Chinese cuisine. Our chefs bring decades of experience, blending traditional flavors with modern innovation.

We use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to create memorable dining experiences. Each dish tells a story of passion, creativity, and respect for culinary traditions.
